## Is day-by-day planning too rigid?

No. A day-by-day guide gives structure without forcing every hour to stay fixed. It helps you understand what is planned so changes are easier to make.

## What should each travel day include?

Useful days usually include the main plan, map context, stays, transport, meals, reservation notes, tickets, open decisions, and practical tips.

## Why does offline access matter for itineraries?

Travel often happens with weak or expensive connectivity. Offline access keeps the plan available when you need addresses, timing, or booking details.

## Can co-travelers use the guide?

Yes. A shared guide lets other travelers understand the day without reading through the full planning history.
