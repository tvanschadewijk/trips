## Does OurTrips have its own travel agent?

Yes. OurTrips can create a trip from the beginning and keep editing the itinerary through the attached trip chat.

## What happens after the first draft is created?

You can open the trip, review each day, share it, save it offline, and keep refining the guide through chat.

## Can the agent edit specific parts of a trip?

Yes. It can make targeted updates to days, hotels, meals, transport, activities, maps, notes, and open decisions.

## Are external agents still supported?

Yes. The remote MCP connector still works for people who already plan in Claude, Codex, or another compatible agent, but it is optional.
