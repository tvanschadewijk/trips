## Do I need Claude, Cowork, or Codex to use OurTrips?

No. You can start a trip directly inside OurTrips. The external connector is optional for people who already plan in another agent.

## What kind of information can I collect in OurTrips?

You can bring dates, travelers, bookings, preferences, notes, reference material, open decisions, and practical constraints such as arrival times, budget, pace, and must-do places.

## Why is day-by-day planning important?

When you are traveling, you usually need the details for today: where to go, where you sleep, what is booked, and what needs attention. A day-by-day guide keeps that information close.

## Can I keep editing a trip after the first draft?

Yes. The trip chat stays attached to the itinerary, so you can keep refining days, meals, hotels, transport, maps, and practical notes.
