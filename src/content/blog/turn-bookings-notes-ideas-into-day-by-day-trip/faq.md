## What should I prepare before starting a trip?

You can start with only destination and dates, but the draft improves when you add bookings, notes, references, budget, pace, must-do places, and constraints.

## Does OurTrips replace travel research?

It helps organize and shape research into a usable guide. You can still bring your own ideas, links, reservations, and preferences so the plan reflects the trip you actually want.

## Can OurTrips handle open decisions?

Yes. Open hotel choices, reservation status, activity ideas, and undecided days can stay visible instead of being hidden in a chat or note.

## Can I share the guide with other travelers?

Yes. Trips can be shared so co-travelers can view the plan without needing to read through your planning notes.
